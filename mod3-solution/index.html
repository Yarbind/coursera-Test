Chicken

Chicken is preferabbly the most consumed food stuff around the world. The evolution of chicken as a menu-list came after the second world war. Moreover, after the downset of worldwar II numerous industries, food processing factories and conusmer-realted service providers started consuming chicken in a enormous Quantity. At some point the consumption was increased in such a way that industries have to opt for other methods of chicken consumption. I started eating chicken after my seventh class, before that mutton was the only meat my tongue has ever tasted. Nowadays, there are numerous recipies of chicken available around the world. The varities differs from locations to locations. In USA people prefer grilled chicken but in India or South East Asian region people prefer butter chicken, Qorma chicken and grilled chicken.Mine favourite is probably fish, but in case of chicken I go with crispy one and with gravy chicken decorated by green chillies on the top. Let me tell you one reciepie of chicken dish which i preferably know or atlest cook by myself. It's called chicken roast and believe me it is tasty and watery for sure. I can feel the saliva released by those taste buds in my tongue. So, let's get started. Firstly you need to have 500gm of freshly cut chicken, boneless or whole. try to have leg piece or chest region. wash them if required and then put them in refrigerator. Buy 250gm of youghurt, or lemon will work fine, but go for curd if you can. Green chillies, Beasan( peanuts/chickpeas flour), ointment, paste of red chillies, garlic,ginger, and tomatoes. grind them properly to make paste. after paste is made put paste, curd, and some ointment in the freshly cut chicken and mix them properly. aster mixing is done add flour and mix it again. Then leave the mixture for around 45 to 60 minutes in the refrigerator.
